Help: Add Stock

Description:
The add stock functionality allows you to create a new stock record in the system.

How to Use:
Navigate to the add stock page.
Fill in the required stock details in the form.
Click "Capture Stock" to save the new stock record.

Required Inputs:
Stock Category, Supplier, Stock Name, Quantity Available, Maximum Stock Level, Minimum Stock Level, Reorder Point, Measurement.

Example:

To add a new stock item, navigate to the add stock page, fill in the form with the stock details, and click "Capture Stock".

FAQ:

Q: How do I add a new stock item?
A: Navigate to the add stock page, fill in the required stock details in the form, and click "Capture Stock".

Q: What information do I need to add a new stock item?
A: You need to provide the Stock Category, Supplier, Stock Name, Quantity Available, Maximum Stock Level, Minimum Stock Level, Reorder Point, and Measurement.

Q: Why can't I submit the form?
A: Ensure all required fields are filled out correctly. The "Capture Stock" button will be enabled only if the form is valid.

Q: How do I know if the stock was added successfully?
A: Upon successful addition, a confirmation message will be displayed. If there is an error, an error message will appear.

Q: What should I do if I encounter an error while adding stock?
A: Check the error message for details and ensure all required fields are correctly filled. If the issue persists, contact support.
