Help: Capture Employee Daily Hours

Description:
The capture functionality allows you to record daily hours worked by an employee.

How to Use:
Navigate to the "Capture Daily Hours" page.
Select the employee from the list.
Enter the date and hours worked.
Click "Save" to record the daily hours.

Required Inputs:
Employee selection, Date, Hours worked.

Example:

To capture daily hours, navigate to the "Capture Daily Hours" page, select the employee, enter the date and hours worked, and click "Save".

FAQ:

Q: How do I capture daily hours for an employee?
A: Navigate to the "Capture Daily Hours" page, select the employee from the list, enter the date and hours worked, and click "Save".

Q: What information is required to capture daily hours?
A: The required information includes employee selection, date, and hours worked.

Q: Why is my entry being rejected?
A: Ensure that the date is in the correct format and the hours worked do not exceed any specified limits.

Q: How do I save the captured daily hours?
A: Click the "Save" button after entering the required information. The hours will be recorded if the form is valid.

Q: What should I do if I need to correct a mistake in the daily hours entry?
A: Locate the entry in the daily hours list and use the edit or delete functionality to correct the mistake.
