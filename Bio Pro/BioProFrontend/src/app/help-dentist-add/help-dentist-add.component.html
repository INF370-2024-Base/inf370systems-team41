Help: Add Dentist

Description:
The Add Dentist functionality allows you to add a new dentist to the system.

How to Use:
Click on the "Add Dentist" button.
Fill in the required details in the form, including the surname, name, contact details, and address.
Click "Submit" to save the new dentist.

Required Inputs:
Surname, Name, Contact Details, Address.

Example:

To add a new dentist, click the "Add Dentist" button, fill in the form with the required details, and click "Submit".

FAQs:

Q: How do I add a new dentist?
A: Navigate to the add dentist page, fill in the required details including the surname, name, contact details, and address, and click "Submit".

Q: What information is needed to add a dentist?
A: You need to provide the surname, name, contact details, and address.

Q: Why can't I add a dentist?
A: Ensure that all required fields are filled in correctly and the form is valid. If there is still an issue, contact support.
