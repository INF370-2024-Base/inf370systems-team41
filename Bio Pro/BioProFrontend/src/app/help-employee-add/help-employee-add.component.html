Help: Add Employee

Description:
The add functionality allows you to create a new employee record in the system.

How to Use:
Click on the "Add Employee" button.
Fill in the required employee details in the form.
Click "Save" to save the new employee record.

Required Inputs:
First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, Job Title.

Example:

To add a new employee, click the "Add Employee" button, fill in the form with the employee's details, and click "Save".

FAQ:

Q: How do I add a new employee to the system?
A: Navigate to the "Add Employee" page, fill in the required fields, and click "Save".

Q: What information is required to add an employee?
A: The required information includes First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, Job Title.

Q: Why is the form not accepting my input?
A: Ensure that the email is in a valid format, the phone number meets any specified format requirements, and all required fields are filled out.

Q: How do I save the new employee's details?
A: Click the "Save" button at the bottom of the form. If the form is valid, the employee will be added to the system.

Q: What should I do if I accidentally close the form before saving?
A: If the form is closed before saving, the entered information will be lost. Reopen the form and re-enter the necessary details.
