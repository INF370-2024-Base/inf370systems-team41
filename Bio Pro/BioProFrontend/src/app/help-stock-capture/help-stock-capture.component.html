Help: Capture Stock

Description:
The capture stock functionality allows you to add new quantities to existing stock items.

How to Use:
Navigate to the capture stock page.
Select the stock item and enter the quantity to be added.
Click "Submit" to update the stock quantity.

Required Inputs:
Stock Item, Quantity Added.

Example:

To capture new stock, navigate to the capture stock page, select the stock item, enter the quantity to be added, and click "Submit".

FAQ:

Q: How do I capture new stock?
A: Navigate to the capture stock page, select the stock item, enter the quantity to be added, and click "Submit".

Q: What information do I need to capture new stock?
A: You need to select the stock item and enter the quantity to be added.

Q: Why can't I submit the form?
A: Ensure all required fields are filled out correctly. The "Submit" button will be enabled only if the form is valid.

Q: How do I know if the stock was captured successfully?
A: Upon successful capture, a confirmation message will be displayed. If there is an error, an error message will appear.

Q: What should I do if I encounter an error while capturing stock?
A: Check the error message for details and ensure all required fields are correctly filled. If the issue persists, contact support.
