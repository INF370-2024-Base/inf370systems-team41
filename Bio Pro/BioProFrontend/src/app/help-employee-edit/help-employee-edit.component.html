Help: Edit Employee

Description:
The edit functionality allows you to update an existing employee's details.

How to Use:
Navigate to the employee list.
Find the employee you want to edit and click the "Edit" button.
Update the necessary details in the form.
Click "Save" to update the employee's information.

Required Inputs:
First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, Job Title.

Example:

To edit an employee, navigate to the employee list, find the employee, click "Edit", update the details, and click "Save".

FAQ:

Q: How do I find and edit an existing employee's information?
A: Navigate to the employee list, use the search functionality to find the desired employee, click the "Edit" button, update the necessary details, and click "Save".

Q: Why can't I edit certain fields?
A: Some fields, such as Employee ID, may be non-editable due to system restrictions.

Q: How do I save my changes?
A: After editing the desired fields, click the "Save" button to update the employee's information.

Q: What should I do if I see an error message when saving changes?
A: Check for validation errors, such as incorrect email or phone format. Correct any errors and try saving again.

Q: How do I cancel my changes and revert to the original data?
A: Click the "Cancel" button to discard any changes and revert to the original employee data.
