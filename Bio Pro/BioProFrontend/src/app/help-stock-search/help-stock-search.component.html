Help: Search Stock

Description:
The search stock functionality allows you to find specific stock records within the system.

How to Use:
Navigate to the search stock page.
Enter the search criteria (e.g., stock name, category, supplier) in the search field.
Click "Search" to display the matching stock records.

Possible Search Criteria:
Stock Name, Stock Category, Supplier.

Example:

To search for stock, navigate to the search stock page, enter "Laptop" in the search field, and click "Search". The results will display all stocks matching the criteria.

FAQ:

Q: How do I search for stock?
A: Navigate to the search stock page, enter the relevant search criteria (e.g., stock name, category, supplier) in the search field, and click "Search".

Q: What information can I use to search for stock?
A: You can search by Stock Name, Stock Category, and Supplier.

Q: Why are no results appearing when I perform a search?
A: Ensure that the search criteria entered are correct and relevant to the existing stock records. Broaden the search criteria if necessary.
