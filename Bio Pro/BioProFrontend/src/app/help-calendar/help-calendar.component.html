Overview

The Calendar section allows users to view and manage events related to both patients and procedural timelines. You can see all scheduled events for specific dates, including patient-related activities and dental procedures, and filter by event type.

Common Questions

  • How do I view Patient events on the calendar?

    Patient events, such as scheduled appointments or follow-ups, are displayed in the calendar. You can view them in the weekly or monthly view. Simply click on the event for detailed information.

  • How can I add a new Patient event?

    If you have the necessary permissions, click the "Add Event" button. Fill in the patient details, select the date and time, and save the event to schedule a new patient appointment.

  • Can I edit or delete a Patient event?

    Yes, if you have the appropriate permissions, you can edit or delete a Patient event by clicking on the event and selecting the desired action from the options provided.

  • How do I view Procedural events?

    Procedural events, including dental procedures, are listed under the "Procedural Timeline" section of the calendar. You can filter events by specific timelines or view all scheduled procedures for a given time frame.

  • How can I add a new Procedural event?

    To add a new Procedural event, click the "Add Event" button, select "Procedure" from the event type dropdown, fill in the necessary details, and save the event to schedule the procedure.

  • Can I associate a Procedural event with a specific Patient?

    Yes, when creating or editing a Procedural event, you can associate it with a specific Patient by selecting the patient from the dropdown menu in the event details form.

  • How can I search for specific events?

    Use the search bar at the top of the calendar page to filter events by name, patient, or description. You can also use the dropdown to filter procedural events by type or timeline.

  • Can I customize the calendar view?

    Yes, you can switch between "Week View" and "Month View" using the view selector. Additionally, you can filter events by type or timeline to focus on specific information.

  • What do the different event colors represent?

    Each event is color-coded based on its type. Patient events and procedural events are visually distinguished by different colors to help you quickly identify them on the calendar.

  • How do I navigate between different dates?

    Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons to move between weeks or months. The "Today" button will quickly return you to the current date.

  • Is there a way to filter events by specific criteria?

    Yes, you can use the dropdown menus to filter events by procedural timelines or event types, allowing you to focus on relevant events.

  • How do I view detailed information about an event?

    Click on the event in the calendar to open a detailed view. This will provide more information about the event, including associated patient details and procedural information.

  • Can I export calendar events?

    Currently, exporting calendar events is not supported directly from the calendar. If you need to export data, please contact the support team for assistance.

  • How often is the calendar data updated?

    The calendar data refreshes automatically when you navigate between views or perform actions like adding or editing events. For the latest updates, you can also manually refresh the page.

  • Are there any permissions required to access certain calendar features?

    Yes, certain features like adding, editing, or deleting events may require specific user roles or permissions. Ensure you have the necessary access rights to perform these actions.

    Clearing your browser cache can help resolve loading issues. Instructions vary by browser:

    • Edge: Go to Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data.
