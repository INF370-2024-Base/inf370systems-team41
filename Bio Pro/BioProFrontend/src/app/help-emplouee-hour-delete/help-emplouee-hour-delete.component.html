Help: Delete Employee Daily Hours

Description:
The delete functionality allows you to remove a daily hours entry for an employee.

How to Use:
Navigate to the daily hours list.
Find the specific entry you want to delete.
Click the "Delete" button next to the entry.
Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Example:

To delete a daily hours entry, navigate to the daily hours list, find the specific entry, click "Delete", and confirm the action when prompted.

FAQ:

Q: How do I delete a daily hours entry for an employee?
A: Navigate to the daily hours list, find the specific entry you want to delete, click the "Delete" button, and confirm the deletion when prompted.

Q: What happens to the data after I delete a daily hours entry?
A: The entry will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Q: Why can't I delete certain daily hours entries?
A: Ensure there are no dependencies or restrictions preventing deletion.

Q: How do I confirm the deletion of a daily hours entry?
A: A confirmation dialog will appear when you click "Delete." Confirm the action to proceed with the deletion.

Q: What should I do if I delete a daily hours entry by mistake?
A: Contact system support to see if there are any options for data recovery.
