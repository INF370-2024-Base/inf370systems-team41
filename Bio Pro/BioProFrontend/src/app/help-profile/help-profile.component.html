lock Update Password

This section provides guidance on how to update your account password securely.

Common Questions

  • What is required to update my password?

    To update your password, you need to enter your current password, choose a new password, and confirm the new password. Ensure the new password meets the system's security requirements, which include a minimum length of 6 characters, an uppercase letter, and a special character.

  • Why is the password update form locked?

    The form locks after 1.5 minutes of inactivity to ensure security. If locked, you need to wait for 5 minutes before you can attempt to update your password again.

  • What happens if I enter the wrong current password?

    If the current password you entered does not match your account password, the update will fail, and you will receive an error message. Double-check the password and try again.

  • How do I ensure my new password is strong?

    Make sure your new password contains at least 6 characters, including an uppercase letter and a special character (e.g., *, !, #). This helps protect your account from unauthorized access.

  • What if the passwords I enter do not match?

    If your new password and confirm password fields do not match, the system will display an error. You need to ensure both passwords are the same before submitting.

  • Can I update my password if I forget my current password?

    No, you need your current password to update it. If you forget your current password, use the 'Forgot Password' feature to reset it.

  • What happens after I successfully update my password?

    After successfully updating your password, you will be logged out for security reasons. You will need to log in again with your new password.

  • Why does the password update form lock after some time?

    The form locks after 1.5 minutes of inactivity to enhance security. Once locked, the form will be inaccessible for 5 minutes. After the lock duration, you can attempt to update your password again.

  • What should I do if my current password is not recognized?

    If the system does not recognize your current password, double-check the password entered. If you still encounter issues, consider using the 'Forgot Password' feature to reset your password.

  • What happens if the 'Confirm Password' field does not match the new password?

    If the new password and confirm password fields do not match, the system will show an error. You must re-enter the correct password in both fields to proceed with the update.

  • Can I update my password if I forget my current one?

    No, the current password is required to update it. If you forget your current password, use the 'Forgot Password' feature to reset it instead of updating.

  • Will I be logged out after successfully updating my password?

    Yes, once the password is updated successfully, the system will log you out for security reasons. You will need to log back in using your new password to continue accessing the system.

Technical Issues

  • Why is the form not responding after I enter my details?

    If the form becomes unresponsive, this could be due to network connectivity issues. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the problem persists, contact technical support for further assistance.

  • What should I do if I encounter an error while updating my password?

    If an error occurs while updating your password, check if the current password is correct and that the new password meets the system's criteria. If the issue persists, try again or contact technical support.

  • Why can’t I submit the form even though I’ve filled in all the fields?

    Ensure that your new password meets the validation rules: it must contain at least 6 characters, one uppercase letter, and a special character. The confirm password field must also match the new password.

  • What happens if the timer runs out before I submit the form?

    If the timer expires while you're updating your password, the form will lock for 5 minutes. You’ll have to wait for the lock period to end before attempting to update your password again.

  • Why do I keep receiving an error that my new password is invalid?

    If you're receiving an invalid password error, ensure that your new password contains an uppercase letter, a special character, and is at least 6 characters long. Re-enter the password correctly and try again.

  • What should I do if I forget my password and the form is locked?

    If you forget your password and the form is locked, you will need to wait for the lock to expire. If you're still unable to update your password, use the 'Forgot Password' feature to reset your password.

  • What if I experience a system error while updating my password?

    If you encounter a system error, such as a server error, while updating your password, contact support with the error message. They can assist you in resolving the issue and ensuring your password is updated.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Enter your current password

In the 'Old Password' field, enter your current password. This is the password you currently use to log into your account.

 Step 2: Enter a new password

In the 'New Password' field, type a new password that meets the system’s security requirements. The password must contain at least 6 characters, including an uppercase letter and a special character.

 Step 3: Confirm the new password

In the 'Confirm New Password' field, re-enter the new password exactly as you entered it in the previous step. This ensures that both entries match.

 Step 4: Submit the form

Click the 'Update Password' button to submit your new password. The system will check that your current password is correct and that the new password matches the confirmation field.

 Step 5: Wait for confirmation

After submitting, you will receive a confirmation message if your password is updated successfully. If the update fails, review any error messages and retry the process.

 Step 6: Log in with your new password

Once your password is updated, you will be logged out. Log back into the system using your new password to continue accessing your account.

person_add Add Dentist

This section provides assistance on how to add a new dentist within the system.

Common Questions

  • How do I add a new dentist?

    To add a new dentist, navigate to the 'Add Dentist' page by clicking the 'Add Dentist' button on the top right corner of the dentist management page. Fill in the required fields and click 'Add Dentist'.

  • What information is required to add a dentist?

    The required fields are First Name, Last Name, and Contact Details. The Address is optional but recommended.

  • Can I add a dentist without an email address?

    Yes, email is not a required field but it is recommended to have for communication purposes.

  • How can I add a dentist's specialization?

    There is a field for specialization in the 'Add Dentist' form. Make sure to fill it with the dentist's area of expertise.

  • Can I edit the dentist details after adding them?

    Yes, you can edit the dentist details by navigating to the 'Edit Dentist' section.

Technical Issues

  • What happens if I don't fill in all required fields?

    The form will not be submitted, and an error message will be displayed below each missing field indicating that it is required.

  • What if I encounter an error while adding a dentist?

    If you encounter an error, check if all required fields are filled correctly. If the problem persists, contact technical support for further assistance.

  • Why is the 'Add Dentist' button unresponsive?

    This could be due to a script error or browser issue. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact technical support.

  • What should I do if the page does not load?

    Check your internet connection and try reloading the page. If the problem continues, contact technical support.

  • Why am I getting a server error when trying to add a dentist?

    This could be due to server issues or database connectivity problems. Contact technical support for assistance.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Navigate to the 'Dentists' page

Click the 'Dentists' tab in the main navigation menu.

 Step 2: Click the 'Add Dentist' button

Locate the 'Add Dentist' button at the top right corner of the page and click it.

 Step 3: Fill in required fields

Complete the required fields: Surname, First Name, and Contact Details.

 Step 4: Add optional information

Optionally, fill in the Address field for more complete records.

 Step 5: Submit the form

Click the 'Add Dentist' button at the bottom of the form to submit.

 Step 6: Confirm addition

Look for a confirmation message indicating successful addition of the dentist.

 Step 7: Verify the new dentist

Check the dentist management page to see the newly added dentist in the list.

delete Delete Dentist

This section provides assistance on how to delete an existing dentist from the system.

Common Questions

  • How do I delete a dentist's record?

    Navigate to the 'Dentist Management' section, select the dentist you want to delete, and click 'Delete'. Confirm the action to remove the record.

  • Can I delete multiple dentist records at once?

    Currently, records need to be deleted individually. Ensure to confirm the deletion for each record.

  • What if I delete the wrong dentist record?

    Unfortunately, deletion is permanent. Double-check the details before confirming. If you need to restore the data, contact technical support to see if a recent backup can be used.

  • Is there a way to recover a deleted dentist record?

    Once deleted, records cannot be recovered. Ensure you double-check before confirming the deletion.

  • Why can’t I find the dentist record I want to delete?

    Check if you are looking at the correct list or using the search/filter options to locate the specific dentist record.

Technical Issues

  • Why can't I see the 'Delete' button?

    Ensure you have the necessary permissions to delete dentist records. Some user roles may not have this authorization.

  • What should I do if the deletion confirmation dialog does not appear?

    Ensure pop-ups are enabled for the site. Try refreshing the page or using a different browser.

  • Why am I receiving an error when trying to delete a dentist record?

    This could be due to a server or database issue. Contact technical support with the error details.

  • Why is the dentist record still visible after deletion?

    There might be a delay in updating the dentist list. Refresh the page to see if the deletion has been processed.

  • What if I accidentally delete the wrong dentist record?

    Unfortunately, deletion is permanent. To avoid this, double-check the details before confirming the deletion. If you need to restore the data, contact technical support to see if a recent backup can be used.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Access the 'Delete Dentist' Page

Click on the 'Delete Dentist' button in the navigation menu or on the dentist details page.

 Step 2: Confirm Deletion

Review the dentist's details to ensure the correct dentist is selected for deletion.

 Step 3: Click 'Delete'

Click the 'Delete' button in the confirmation dialog to proceed with deletion.

 Step 4: Confirm Deletion

Check for a confirmation message indicating successful deletion of the dentist from the system.
