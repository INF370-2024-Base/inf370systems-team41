Help: Search Employee

Description:
The search functionality allows you to find specific employee records within the system.

How to Use:
Navigate to the employee search page.
Enter the search criteria (e.g., name, job title, department) in the search field.
Click the "Search" button to display the matching employee records.

Possible Search Criteria:
First Name, Last Name, Email, Job Title, Department.

Example:

To search for an employee, navigate to the employee search page, enter "John Doe" in the search field, and click "Search". The results will display all employees matching the criteria.

FAQ:

Q: How do I search for an employee?
A: Navigate to the employee search page, enter the relevant search criteria (e.g., name, job title, department) in the search field, and click "Search".

Q: What information can I use to search for an employee?
A: You can search by First Name, Last Name, Email, Job Title, and Department.

Q: Why are no results appearing when I perform a search?
A: Ensure that the search criteria entered are correct and relevant to the existing employee records. Broaden the search criteria if necessary.

Q: How can I refine my search results?
A: Use more specific search criteria or combinations of criteria to narrow down the search results.

Q: What should I do if I can't find the employee I'm looking for?
A: Verify the accuracy of the search criteria. If the employee is still not found, ensure that the employee record exists in the system.
