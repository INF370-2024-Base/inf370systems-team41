Help: Delete Employee

Description:
The delete functionality allows you to remove an employee's record from the system.

How to Use:
Navigate to the employee list.
Find the employee you want to delete and click the "Delete" button.
Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Example:

To delete an employee, navigate to the employee list, find the employee, click "Delete", and confirm the action when prompted.

FAQ:

Q: How do I delete an employee from the system?
A: Navigate to the employee list, find the employee you want to delete, click the "Delete" button, and confirm the deletion when prompted.

Q: What happens to the employee's data after deletion?
A: The employee's data will be permanently removed from the system and cannot be recovered.

Q: Why can't I delete certain employee records?
A: There may be dependencies or restrictions preventing deletion. Ensure there are no related records that require the employee's data.

Q: How do I confirm the deletion of an employee?
A: A confirmation dialog will appear when you click "Delete." Confirm the action to proceed with the deletion.

Q: What should I do if I delete an employee by mistake?
A: Contact system support to see if there are any options for data recovery.
