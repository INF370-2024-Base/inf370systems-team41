Help: Edit Dentist

Description:
The Edit Dentist functionality allows you to modify the details of an existing dentist in the system.

How to Use:
Click on the "Edit" button next to the dentist you want to modify.
Update the required details in the form.
Click "Save" to save the changes.

Required Inputs:
Surname, Name, Contact Details, Address.

Example:

To edit a dentist, click the "Edit" button, update the details, and click "Save".

FAQs:

Q: How do I edit a dentist?
A: Navigate to the dentist profile, click the "Edit" button, update the details, and click "Save".

Q: What information can I edit?
A: You can edit the surname, name, contact details, and address.

Q: Why can't I edit a dentist?
A: Ensure that all required fields are filled in correctly and the form is valid. If there is still an issue, contact support.
