Help: Search Dentist

Description:
The Search Dentist functionality allows you to search for dentists in the system by various criteria.

How to Use:
Enter the search criteria in the search bar.
Click "Search" to find dentists that match the criteria.

Search Criteria:
First Name, Last Name, Contact Detail, Address.

Example:

To search for a dentist, enter the desired criteria in the search bar and click "Search".

FAQs:

Q: How do I search for a dentist?
A: Enter the search criteria in the search bar and click "Search".

Q: What criteria can I use to search for a dentist?
A: You can search by first name, last name, contact detail, or address.

Q: Why can't I find the dentist I'm looking for?
A: Ensure that the search criteria are correct and the dentist is in the system. If there is still an issue, contact support.
