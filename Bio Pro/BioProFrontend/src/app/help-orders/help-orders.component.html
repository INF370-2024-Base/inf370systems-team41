add_circle_outline New Orders

This section provides assistance on how to manage and navigate orders within the system.

Common Questions

  • How do I add a new order?

    To add a new order, navigate to the 'Add Order' page and fill out the required fields. Click 'Submit' to save the order.

  • What information is required to create a new order?

    You need to provide details such as customer information, order items, and delivery dates. Ensure all mandatory fields are filled out.

  • Can I edit an order after submitting it?

    Yes, you can edit an order by navigating to the 'Orders' page, selecting the order you want to edit, and updating the necessary information.

  • How do I cancel an order?

    To cancel an order, go to the 'Orders' page, select the order you want to cancel, and click the 'Cancel Order' button.

  • How do I track the status of an order?

    Order statuses can be tracked on the 'Orders' page where each order will display its current status (e.g., pending, shipped, delivered).

Technical Issues

  • I'm unable to submit an order. What should I do?

    If you encounter issues submitting an order, ensure all required fields are filled out correctly. If the problem persists, contact support.

  • Why am I seeing an error message when adding an order?

    Error messages usually indicate missing or incorrect information. Check the form for any highlighted fields and correct the errors.

  • Why is the order page not loading?

    This could be due to a network issue or server downtime. Refresh the page or try again later. If the issue persists, contact support.

  • Why is my order not showing up in the list after submission?

    There might be a delay in updating the order list. Try refreshing the page or checking back later. If the issue continues, contact support.

  • Why am I unable to edit an order?

    Editing an order may be restricted based on its status or user permissions. Ensure you have the necessary permissions or contact an administrator.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Access the 'Add Order' Page

Click on the 'Add Order' button on the main orders page or use the navigation menu to go to the 'Add Order' page.

 Step 2: Fill Out Order Details

Provide all necessary information including customer details, order items, and delivery dates. Make sure all mandatory fields are completed.

 Step 3: Submit the Order

Review the order details and click 'Submit' to save the order. If there are any errors, they will be highlighted for correction.

folder_open Open Orders

This section offers guidance on understanding and tracking the timeline of orders from creation to completion.

Common Questions

  • How do I view open orders?

    Navigate to the 'Open Orders' page to see a list of all current open orders along with their details.

  • Can I edit an open order?

    Yes, you can edit an open order by selecting it from the list and updating the necessary information.

  • What information is displayed for each open order?

    Each open order shows its ID, current status, and other relevant details.

  • How do I track the progress of an open order?

    The 'Open Orders' page provides real-time updates on the status of each order.

  • How do I filter open orders?

    You can use the filter options on the 'Open Orders' page to narrow down the list based on specific criteria such as date range or status.

  • Can I export the list of open orders?

    Yes, there is an export option on the 'Open Orders' page that allows you to download the list in various formats such as CSV or PDF.

Technical Issues

  • Why can't I see any open orders?

    This could be due to a network issue or there might be no open orders at the moment. Try refreshing the page or check back later.

  • Why is the open order information not updating?

    This might be due to a delay in the system. Please wait for a few moments and try refreshing the page.

  • Why am I unable to edit an open order?

    Editing an order may be restricted based on its status or user permissions. Ensure you have the necessary permissions or contact an administrator.

  • Why is the order list taking too long to load?

    This could be due to a slow network connection or high server load. Try reloading the page or checking your network connection.

  • Why do I get a "403 Forbidden" error when accessing the open orders page?

    This error may occur if you don't have the necessary permissions to view open orders. Contact your administrator for access.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Access the 'Open Orders' Page

Click on the 'Open Orders' button on the main navigation menu to go to the 'Open Orders' page.

 Step 2: Review the Open Orders List

Review the list of open orders, each displaying the order ID, current status, and other relevant details.

 Step 3: Select an Order to View Details

Click on an order to view its detailed information, including customer details, order items, and the order's progress.
