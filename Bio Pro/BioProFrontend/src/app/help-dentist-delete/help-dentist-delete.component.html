Help: Delete Dentist

Description:
The delete functionality allows you to remove a dentist record from the system.

How to Use:
Click on the "Delete" button next to the dentist you want to remove.
Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Required Inputs:
No inputs required, just confirmation.

Restrictions:
Deletion is irreversible. Once deleted, the dentist record cannot be recovered.

Are you sure you want to delete this dentist?

Example:

To delete a dentist, click the "Delete" button next to their name, and confirm the action in the dialog.
