Help: Delete Dentist

Description:
The Delete Dentist functionality allows you to remove a dentist from the system.

How to Use:
Click on the "Delete" button next to the dentist you want to remove.
Confirm the deletion in the prompt that appears.

Example:

To delete a dentist, click the "Delete" button next to the dentist's name and confirm the deletion in the prompt.

FAQs:

Q: How do I delete a dentist?
A: Click the "Delete" button next to the dentist's name and confirm the deletion in the prompt.

Q: Why can't I delete a dentist?
A: Ensure that you have the necessary permissions and that the dentist is not linked to any active records. If there is still an issue, contact support.

Q: Can I recover a deleted dentist?
A: Once a dentist is deleted, it cannot be recovered. Ensure you have backed up any necessary information before deletion.
