add Add Stock

This section provides assistance on how to add new stock items to the system.

Common Questions

  • How do I add new stock?

    Navigate to the 'Add Stock' page, fill in the required information such as stock category, supplier, stock name, and quantity. Ensure all fields are completed accurately and then click 'Capture Stock' to add the new item.

  • What information is required to add new stock?

    You will need to provide the stock category, supplier, stock name, quantity available, maximum stock level, minimum stock level, reorder point, and measurement.

Technical Issues

  • Why can't I submit the form?

    Ensure all required fields are filled out correctly and the form is valid. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or using a different browser. Contact technical support if the issue continues.

  • Why are the stock categories or suppliers not loading?

    Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, it might be due to a server problem. Contact technical support for further assistance.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Access the 'Add Stock' Page

Click on the 'Add Stock' button in the navigation menu to go to the 'Add Stock' page.

 Step 2: Fill in Stock Details

Provide the necessary stock details including stock category, supplier, stock name, and quantity available.

 Step 3: Complete Additional Information

Fill in additional information such as maximum stock level, minimum stock level, reorder point, and measurement.

 Step 4: Submit the Form

Click the 'Capture Stock' button to add the new stock item. A confirmation message will appear if the stock is successfully added.

add_box Capture Stock

This section provides assistance on how to capture stock details in the system.

Common Questions

  • How do I capture new stock?

    Navigate to the 'Capture Stock' page, select the stock item, and provide the quantity added. Ensure all fields are completed accurately and then click 'Submit' to capture the stock.

  • What information is required to capture stock?

    You will need to provide the stock name and the amount added.

Technical Issues

  • Why can't I submit the form?

    Ensure all required fields are filled out correctly and the form is valid. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page or using a different browser. Contact technical support if the issue continues.

  • Why is the stock not updating?

    Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, it might be due to a server problem. Contact technical support for further assistance.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Access the 'Capture Stock' Page

Click on the 'Capture Stock' button in the navigation menu to go to the 'Capture Stock' page.

 Step 2: Select the Stock Item

Select the stock item from the list of available stock items.

 Step 3: Provide Quantity Added

Enter the quantity of stock added in the appropriate field.

 Step 4: Submit the Form

Click the 'Submit' button to capture the stock. A confirmation message will appear if the stock is successfully captured.

search Search Stock

This section provides assistance on how to search for stock items in the system.

Common Questions

  • How do I search for stock?

    Navigate to the 'Search Stock' page, enter the stock name or other identifying information in the search bar, and click the search button. The results will display the matching stock items.

  • What information can I use to search for stock?

    You can search for stock items using the stock name, category, or supplier.

Technical Issues

  • Why are my search results not displaying?

    Ensure you have entered valid search criteria and try again. If the issue persists, it might be due to a server problem or an issue with the search functionality. Contact technical support for further assistance.

  • Why is the search function not working?

    Check your internet connection and try refreshing the page. If the problem continues, there might be a technical issue with the search function. Contact technical support for help.

Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: Access the 'Search Stock' Page

Click on the 'Search Stock' button in the navigation menu to go to the 'Search Stock' page.

 Step 2: Enter Search Criteria

Enter the stock name, category, or supplier in the search bar.

 Step 3: Click the Search Button

Click the search button to view the results. Matching stock items will be displayed below the search bar.
